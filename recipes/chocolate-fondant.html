Chocolate Fondant

Description

Simple and quick chocolate fondant recipe to make in a pinch

Ingredients

  • 200g of dark chocolate
  • 200g of wheat
  • 150g of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 125g of butter
  • a splash of whatever milk you like to liquify it a bit

Steps

  1. Melt the butter and chocolate in a bit of water in a pot on light fire
  2. Once completely add sugar and eggs
  3. Add wheat
  4. Pour mix in backing dish coated with butter
  5. Bake for 12 minutes in a preheated oven
  6. Let it cool down and enjoy!
